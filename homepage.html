VulnerUtility

VulnerUtility is an imaginary cyber security company and penetration testing utility. It's goal is to aide security researchers in their quest of discovering, analyzing, testing, documenting, and ultimately patching vulnerabiltiies in systems.

This website is named after the coined-term "VulnerUtility." However, VulnerUtility doesn't yet exist in real life. Our main goal is to encourage others about the different types of cyber security, along with the dangers associated with them.

Our hope is that, with freely-distributed knowledge, users will have a better idea of the reality of the digital world, and what can be done to mitigate those risks.

That's where VulnerUtility comes in. We provide services that range from free-knowledge distribution, to full-on, enterprise information security analysis and remediation.

This is our Mission

VulnerUtility is a hypothetical software tool. It's imaginary purpose is to aide security researchers, ethical hackers, pentesters, and other white hat hackers in the process of identifying, researching, exploiting, securing, and documenting vulnerabilties in web applications and other software programs.
