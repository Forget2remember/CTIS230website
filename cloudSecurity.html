Cloud Security

Cloud security is one of the newer, more modern flavors of cybersecurity to enter the atmosphere. Basically used as a medium for data storage and retrieval, cloud computing offers numerous advantages and easy solutions for any sort of computing job. Whether it be acquiring computer infrastructure, software, or platforms as a service, anyone now has the power to fulfill computing visions that they were once unable to obtain.

But with this power comes great responsibility. Because they're maintaining the data of their customers, cloud operators are on constant pressure to maintain the most capable security.

Cloud services can be operated by public, private, or hybrid cloud providers. A public cloud service means that user data and the applications being used, are hosted with a third party. This is on the other end of the spectrum of traditional IT, where most of our information was stored within a self-controlled network.
